Celery & Ranch

Description

Not too delicious, but it is what it is.

Ingredients

  • Celery
  • Ranch Dressing

Steps

  1. Wash the celery. Who knows where it's been.
  2. Break the celery into sizes that fit best into your lil' hands.
  3. Pour some ranch into one (maybe more than one) bowl.
  4. Dip the celery into the ranch and enjoy it I guess.
