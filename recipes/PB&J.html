PB&J

Description

Truly the most reliable sandwich. Could easily sustain a minimum of 40 hungry brutes stranded in the desert.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Toast that bread, baby. You're worth it.
  2. Spread peanut butter on bread thicc as you like.
  3. Spread jelly on the other piece of bread. Let's not mix things up.
  4. Put the pieces of bread (spread side) together. Enjoy hungry boy you earned it.
