A Good Smoothie

Description

Solid beverage. Great post-workout drink to get your juice back.

Ingredients

  • Frozen fruit
  • Almond Milk
  • Protein Powder

Steps

  1. Put everything in the most powerful blender you can afford.
  2. Press the "on" button. Cover your ears and pray. P.S. Watch those fingers silly!
  3. Run the blender until the smoothie is smooth.
  4. Pour into a glass or drink it straight out of the blender like a criminal.
